Are You Traveling Over Troubled Bridges?

Some 118 sections of NYC bridges — which include overpasses, raised roadways and spans that go over another road, railway or bike path — were found by inspectors to be in poor condition in 2024. See how the bridges you use fared. .

Bridge Components

  • The deck — The road, train tracks or walkways over a bridge
  • The superstructure — The part visible above the deck. The girders, cables and more on top.
  • The substructure — This is generally the part below the deck, including piers going into the ground.
  • The scour — For bridges with piers in water, this measures erosion underneath those piers.
  • The culvert — A tunnel under a bridge that allows water to pass through.
